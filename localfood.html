Local Delicacies

Jakarta's Most Favorites Delicacies

Fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk and spices. It's typically served with various side dishes like fried chicken, tempeh, shredded omelet, and a special peanut sauce. It's a very popular breakfast dish.

Nasi Uduk

A rich and creamy soup made with beef or beef offal in a coconut milk or milk broth. It's full of spices and served with sliced tomatoes and potatoes.

Soto Betawi

A vegetarian dish made with rice cakes, tofu, vermicelli, bean sprouts, and cucumber, all drenched in a sweet and spicy peanut sauce.
